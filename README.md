# My Korean Name

A fun web app that generates a personalized Korean name based on your name and birthday.

## How it works

Enter your name and date of birth, and the app creates a two-syllable Korean name for you:

- The **first syllable** is derived from the sound of your name (supports English and Japanese input)
- The **second syllable** is chosen based on your birth month, each carrying a unique meaning written in Hanja (Chinese characters used in Korean)

Your result includes the Korean name, its Hanja, romanized pronunciation, meaning, and a short personality description based on your birth season.

## Features

- **IP-based language detection** — automatically displays in English or Japanese depending on your location, with browser language as fallback
- **Shareable results** — copy the link or share directly to X (Twitter)
- No account or install needed — runs entirely in the browser

## Tech

Plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No frameworks, no build step.
