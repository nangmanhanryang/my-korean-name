Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how My Korean Name ("we", "the site") handles information when you use our service at this website.

Information We Do Not Collect

The name and date of birth you enter into the form are processed entirely within your browser. This information is never sent to our servers, never stored in a database, and never transmitted to any third party by us. Once you close or navigate away, it is gone.

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

We store one item in your browser's localStorage: your chosen display theme (light or dark mode). This preference never leaves your device and contains no personal information.

Advertising — Google AdSense

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense is operated by Google LLC. When you visit this site, Google may use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to collect information about your browser and online activity in order to show you relevant ads.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and other sites on the Internet. The information collected does not identify you personally by name.

You can learn more about how Google uses data when you use partner sites by visiting Google's Privacy & Terms .

Opting Out of Personalized Ads

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings . Alternatively, you can opt out of third-party vendor use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting aboutads.info .

Cookies

We do not set any first-party cookies ourselves. Google AdSense may set third-party cookies on your browser as described above. Most browsers allow you to control or disable cookies through their settings.

Third-Party Links

The "Share on X" button on result pages links to Twitter/X. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies before interacting with their platforms.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
